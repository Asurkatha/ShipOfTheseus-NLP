Pegasus coverage changes Recall by 0.329 points and Precision by 0.166 points when moving from pegasus(slight) to pegasus(full). If the Recall gap is larger, then broader sentence coverage mainly increases entity dropping; if the Precision gap is larger, then broader coverage mainly increases hallucinated entities. The largest dataset-level Recall gap appears in sci_gen (0.393), while the largest Precision gap appears in sci_gen (0.201). That indicates whether the pattern is stable or domain-sensitive: news, reviews, and scientific text often differ in entity density, so full-coverage paraphrasing may erode named entities more strongly in some domains than others.
